Introduction

[app image]

Snapit, a fun and simple digital assets management tool for your mobile device. We have taken photos, videos more today than ever in human history. To manage digital assets have not been an easy task. With Snapit, this can ever been simpler.

The Story

[person portrait]
Monica is 30 year old stay home mother with 2 beautiful daughters. She has a been copywriter freelancer for the past couple. Ever since her first daughter was born, she take the advantage of her smart phone record all the precious memory with her family. As time goes by, she found that her digital asset growth ‘exponentially’ and become unmanageable; She is desperate to find a tool to help her organize it….
[person portrait]
Dave is amateur journalism. He loves to use his many mobile applications to take photos, videos, audio and note for anything he found interested. However, to search through his digital assets to find something he need quickly is always a tedious task because they are scattering in many place. He needs a better tool that is easy to use and quickly to search for the digital assets he need…

Story continue...

Monica and Dave searched through the internet to find the right tool and they have try: Evernote, Picasa, Instangram etc; however none of them provide a unified solution for what they looking for.

Opportunity

[quote: "CAN I HAVE ONE APP TO DO ALL THESE STUFFS FOR ME"]

It turns out there are many smartphone users facing similar problems like Monica and Dave. Most of the mobile applications lack of features that users are looking for. While one application can do one thing but can’t do another; therefore, people usually end up using multiple applications to store their digital assets.
